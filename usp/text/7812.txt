Members of Prochlorophyta, a proposed new division of procaryotes, are characterized by the combination of a procaryotic cell structure and the presence of chlorophylls a and b., filamentous free living Prochlorothrix hollandica, recently isolated from the freshwater Loosdrecht Lakes, The cell wall morphology of P. hollandica resembles the multilayered fine structure of cyanobacteria and gram-negative bacteria.
