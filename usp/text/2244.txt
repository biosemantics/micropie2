Cells are resistant to penicillin G (10 U), chloramphenicol (30 µg), kanamycin (30 µg), ampicillin (10 µg), tetracycline (30 µg), erythromycin (15 µg), streptomycin (10 µg), polymyxin B (300 U), neomycin (30 µg), gentamicin (10 µg) and carbenicillin (50 µg), but susceptible to bacitracin (10 U) and novobiocin (30 µg).
