The specific net growth rate (K) of the A.ovalisporum population in the lake varied from 0.02 to 0.30 In units day-1 from August to mid-October 1994 and from 0.06 to 0.40 In units day-1 in 1995, corresponding to a doubling time of 2.3-5 days (with a maximum of 21 days) during the stationary phase in 1994, and a doubling time of 1.7-3 days in 1995.
