Sensitive to the following antibiotics (µg per disc, unless otherwise indicated): novobiocin (30), bacitracin (0.04 IU), anisomycin (20), aphidicolin (20) and rifampicin (5); resistant to: erythromycin (15), penicillin G (10 IU), ampicillin (10), chloramphenicol (30), neomycin (30), norfloxacin (10), ciprofloxacin (5), streptomycin (10), kanamycin (30), tetracycline (30), vancomycin (30), gentamicin (10) and nalidixic acid (30)_dot  The polar lipids are PG, PGP-Me and one major GL, which is chromatographically identical to S-DGD-1; three to four minor unidentified glycolipids (GL2–GL5) are also detectable.
