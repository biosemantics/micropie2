Cells are resistant to ampicillin, cephalosporin C, chloramphenicol (25 µg per mL), erythromycin, neomycin, penicillin G and tetracycline, but susceptible to anisomycin, aphidicolin, bacitracin, chloramphenicol (50 µg per mL), novobiocin and rifamycin B.  The ether lipids are diphytanyl derivatives.
