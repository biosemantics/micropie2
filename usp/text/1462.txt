Grows aerobically, but not anaerobically, in a mineral-salts medium containing 0.005% yeast extract, ammonium as the nitrogen source, and suitable carbon and energy sources such as D-glucose, D-galactose, D-fructose, maltose, sucrose, acetate, citrate, fumarate, glycerol, lactate, a-ketoglutarate, malate, succinate or pyruvate.
